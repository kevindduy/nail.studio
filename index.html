Meet Khoi and Yen, the heart and soul behind Nail Studio. Originally from Vietnam, they brought their passion for meticulous nail artistry and genuine, warm hospitality to Palm Harbor.

For Khoi and Yen, this studio is far more than just a business—it is a second home. Over the years, they have built beautiful, lasting relationships with their clients, sharing in their milestones and watching their families grow. They deeply value the loyalty of their customers and believe that every person who walks through the door should be treated like an extension of their own family.
